# Privacy Policy — Browser Organizer

_Last updated: 2026-07-09_

Browser Organizer processes your data **locally**. Tab titles, URLs, bookmarks, and
visit timestamps are read by the extension and passed to a helper program running on
your own computer, which invokes your locally-installed AI CLI. The extension operator
receives none of this data and operates no server that stores it.

- **What we access:** open tab titles/URLs, bookmarks, browsing-history visit times for
  bookmarked URLs, and HTTP status of bookmarked URLs (for dead-link checks).
- **Where it goes:** only to the local helper and your own AI CLI. Your CLI may transmit
  prompts to its provider under your existing subscription and that provider's policy.
- **What we store:** your settings, tab-activity timestamps, and an undo log — all in the
  browser's local storage on your device.
- **What we never do:** sell data, run analytics, or transmit your data to the developer.
